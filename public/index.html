    About the Pomodoro Technique

    What Is the Pomodoro Technique?

    Developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s, the Pomodoro Technique is a renowned time management method. It breaks work into 25-minute intervals, known as Pomodoros, separated by short breaksâ€”inspired by the tomato-shaped kitchen timer Cirillo used in university.

    How It Works

    The technique is straightforward:

    • Choose a task you want to work on.
    • Set the timer for 25 minutes.
    • Work on the task until the timer rings
    • Take a 5-minute break.
    • After four Pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).

    Why Use This App?

    Our app enhances the Pomodoro Technique by offering a customizable timer, automatic break scheduling, and tools to monitor productivity over time. It's designed to help you stay focused, manage time efficiently, and achieve tasks effortlessly.
